How Technology Shapes Our Lives: Exploring the Positive and Negative Effects of Technology

What is technology actually?

Technology refers to the application of scientific Knowledge for practical purposes or application. The use of technology has become very common in our daily life. We all have been surrounded by many technologies. Technology has been developing year to year. Every year or every second new or upgraded technology are being discovered and manufactured. The main purpose of creation of technologies is to make our daily life easier and make our work efficient. There are various kind of technologies, the main types are Mechanical technology, Medical technology, Communication technology, Electronic technology, Industrial technology and Manufacturing technology. The use of technology has been practiced form ancient period. Compared to ancient period now technology has developed very far. The knowledge and resource were very less compared to modern period.

How it can be used for our benefit

The positive impact of Technology are it has expended security, Communication has become easier, technology have helped in medical field and education field. Most of all technology have helped increase human economic output by creating more job opportunity. Technology turns complex task into interesting and simple. Technology has provided us entertainment by creation of television, mobile phone etc.
Technology has helped us by increasing productivity and efficiency. It allows us to perform tasks in less time.

But also beware of its consequences

the main negative impact of technology is addiction. People gets very attached technology that without technology they cannot do anything. Another negative impact is privacy, there is no guarantee that people uses technology for good purposes. Technology can effect people privacy too. Technology also causes pollution. The industrial machines waste are straight dumped in rivers an ocean which leads to water pollution not only that it also create air pollution by releasing industrial harmful smoke in air. Over use of technology can effect peoples life, it can cause physical disorder like eye strain ,Sleep problems ,depression and anxiety.
